# doseestimate
Dose Estimate

Utility for estimate of organ doses and effective dose to patient during diagnostic radiography exposure.

Recommended citations:
1) Kutsen S. A., Khrutchinsky A. A., Minenko V. F., Kukhta T. S., Viarenich K. A. Problem of estimate of dose burdens to patient during radiography examinations in Belarus. In Proceedings of 2nd congress of physicists of Belarus, 3-5 November 2008. Eds. S. Ya. Kilin (chief editor) et al. Minsk. P. 174-175. (in Russian)
2) Minenko V. Assessment of patient doses in X-ray diagnostics / V. Minenko, K. Viarenich, S. Kutsen, A. Khrutchinsky // Int. J. Low Radiation. - 2010. - Vol. 7. - No. 2. - p. 140-148. DOI: 10.1504/IJLR.2010.032817
3) Makarevich K., Minenko V., Kutsen S., Verenich K. (2018). [P052] Influence of X-ray field position on doses in organs and tissues for chest radiography. Physica Medica: European Journal of Medical Physics, 52, 114. DOI: 10.1016/j.ejmp.2018.06.375
